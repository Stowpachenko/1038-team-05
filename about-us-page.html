Learn More About Our History

For more than 40 years we have been delighting our guests with delicious dishes.

1978

Our founder, Peter Manujo, opened his first restaurant, Muelo, in downtown New York.

1997

The Muelo restaurant was renamed into El Gambino and focused on Italian and Spanish cuisine.

2005

We expanded our chain and opened two more El Gambino restaurants, in Boston and Chicago.

2020

TimeToGo named our chain "one of the best Italian restaurant chains in the United States".
